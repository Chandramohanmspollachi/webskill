GENERAL DISCRIPTION

Origin and History

Our Lady of Lourdes Matric. Hr. Sec. School right from the year 1970's came to be recognized for its harmonious blending of the traditional environment with the progressive method of education. Appropriate curricular based activity oriented learning methodology has been the striking feature of the school. Naturally the institution imparts holistic and quality education to all the classes from Pre KG to XII std.
.
Scaling the highest step by step the institution grew every year steadily with an addition of one higher class and in the year 2000 the school reached its full fledged status of a Higher Secondary School.
.
. Growth of our School

The School becomes Higher Secondary School with the inclusion of XII Std. and a strength of nearly 1800 students. The School is affiliated to the Matriculation Board of Education recognized by the Government of Tamilnadu and the medium of instruction is English.
